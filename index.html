Discover Our Restaurant Story

“There's a lot of thought put into which side of the plate a mushroom is on and what’s on top of it and how the colours balance. It's essentially like painting a picture, but what people don't see is the story behind it. Every time we serve a dish, we invite the guest to hear the story behind it. We explain that it’s from our co-owned mushroom farm, and they grow specialty mushrooms that you can't get anywhere else. Knowing that (little bit of information) helps enhance the entire experience because it’s a cool story – and the food tastes better because of how it was grown.”
